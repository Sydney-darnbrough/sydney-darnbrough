assignment i: confounding tools

"Design a 24” × 36” digital poster about your life under COVID 19

Now here’s the catch: you are not allowed to use any adobe software or other software explicitly designed for print design and production in your process. You are free to use any other digital software against its grain."


To create my poster I used a collection of screenshots that I felt best represented my time during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic. This included games and shows that helped occupy me as well as a spreadsheet that tracks some of my spending in 2020. Some of the screenshots are just old forgotten files that I found on my computer. I decided to include these because I felt they were a good expression of feeling somewhat forgotten about.
